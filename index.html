About

This is a site template for accessing the AI/HUB bootcamps based on their general categories.
Each category is broken down into focused topics. Some are focused on theory, while others use actual datasets like MNIST or CIFAR-10 to demonstrate practical applications.

In some cases, links will also connect you with external sites that contain great learning material, such as Medium, or Kaggle. Additionally, each category will contain a 'Basics' lesson with foundational knowledge to get you started!
